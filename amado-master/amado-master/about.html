About Us

           Selfridges is more than a shop. It is a landmark destination and social centre where everyone is welcome. Powered by imagination and filled with creative spirit, we invite collaboration and congregation. From our in-house cinema, restaurants and bars, skate bowl and extraordinary cultural program to our ever-evolving roster of collaborators - artists, designers and innovative new brands - we are a creative playground in which to discover and share new ideas.

           The only store to be named Best Department Store in the World four times, Selfridges is committed to transformational change across all platforms to make the act of shopping responsible, purposeful and riotously fun.

           We continue to honour this rich and storied founding spirit by weaving creative experimentation, activism, and cultural enrichment through our extraordinary customer experience.

OUR AWARDS

IGDS Best Department Store in the World (2010, 2012, 2014, 2018) IGDS World's Best Sustainability Campaign by a Department Store (2016) Global Good Awards Partnership in the Community for Selfridges & ZSL Project Ocean (2017) RIBA London Regional Award for Duke St Entrance (2019) Best Retailer at Positive Luxury Awards (2020)

OUR CAREERS

When it comes to customer experience, no one does it quite like Selfridges, and that’s because our extraordinary team members know how to surprise, amaze and amuse our customers. We’re made up of a range of creative and passionate teams across London, Leicester, Manchester and Birmingham with roles in Retail, Digital, Head Office and Restaurants.

SUPPORTING LOCAL & INDEPENDENT BRANDS

Our expert buying teams scour the globe for under-the-radar fashion talent, emerging sustainable labels, and UK based makers. Our mission is to discover and platform the breakthrough brands who are designing a brighter future.

DIVERSITY, EQUITY & INCLUSION

Harry Gordon Selfridge opened the doors of our Oxford Street store with the manifesto ‘Everyone is Welcome’ - something which we continue to stand by today. We are working to create positive change by increasing the visibility of underrepresented talent in our business and beyond.
